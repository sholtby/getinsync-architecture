Alignment Scorecard

  • Layer 1 (Crawl): Structurally aligned. FK guarantees Consumes relationship. 5 of 8 BA fields ready, 3 of 8 AS fields ready. Three schema gaps to resolve (groups, criticality, change_control role).
  • Layer 2 (Walk): Boundary is correct. GIS tracks tech inventory; Discovery owns infrastructure CIs. No overlap, no conflict.
  • Layer 3 (Data Flows): DP-level FKs correctly align to App Service ↔ App Service. GIS is ahead of SN (richer metadata). Export requires flattening.
  • Layer 4 (Run): GIS IT Services map to Technical Services (Walk), not Business Services (Run). Future scope.

Phase 37 Prerequisites

  • No group entity — 3 SN fields need groups (managed_by_group, support_group, change_control). GIS tracks individuals only.
  • Business criticality — SN needs 1–5 on both BA and AS. GIS has 0–100 on portfolio_assignments (portfolio-scoped, not global).
  • change_control role — Not in deployment_profile_contacts CHECK constraint. Schema change required.
  • Integration metadata loss — method, format, cadence, sensitivity have no home in cmdb_rel_ci. Accept loss or use custom attributes.
  • CSDM export views — vw_csdm_business_app, vw_csdm_service_auto needed to transform GIS model → SN payload.
